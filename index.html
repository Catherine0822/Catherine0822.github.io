ABOUT ME

Hi! I am Catherine Yu, a UX researcher & data analyst.

I am passionate about uncovering user needs from the digital footprints users leave and the mass amount of data.

With 3 years of hands-on experience, I specialize in collecting user data through surveys, usability testing, and behavioral experiments, as well as analyzing and interpreting data. My expertise in quantitative research has contributed to various commercial and academic projects, including human-centric AI, AI for seniors, health care apps, , and messaging apps (Slack).
